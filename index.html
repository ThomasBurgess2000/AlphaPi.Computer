AlphaPi Computer

The distraction-free writing tool that doesn't break your budget like the Freewrite.

Here's how it stacks up against the Freewrite:

  • Customizable - have a keyboard you already love? Type away. Have extra USB battery packs? Power on.
  • Portable - weighs less than a pound (compared to four pounds)
  • Hackable - the software is open-source and ready for modding.
  • Budgetable - you can build the AlphaPi yourself for less than 25% of the cost of a Freewrite.
